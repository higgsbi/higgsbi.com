Blog

Historically, I have used more modern platforms for sharing thoughts, but these considering these sites are predatory, addicting, all consuming monstrosities, I am not a fan. These being your usual suspects: Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, and the like.

After I stopped using these platforms, I naively tried "better" alternatives in the Fediverse. Unfortunately, while these websites are slimmer and feature a better person to bot ratio, they are still not featuring inspiring displays of human connection and quality information sharing that I want. At this point, I am of the opionion there are three ideal forms of communication (in descending order of quality):

  1. Person to person, preferably surrounded by nature
  2. Video call
  3. Email, essay response, or other personal and long form messaging software

This page is an optimistic pursuit of communication method #3. I love reading genuine thoughts of other people on their own blogs, and wish to enjoy the simplistic design of the early internet. So here we are. If you want to read my thoughts, feel free. If you want to comment, question, or explain how silly I am, use my email. I try to respond when I can.

Technology

The infrastructure for this blog is purposefully as simple as possible: plain html and some css, written by hand. I had created a markdown to html generator a while back with pandoc and a nice Javascript UI, but that did not really seem to save any time. In fact, I spent more time tinkering with it than actually writing anything. Writing HTML is not hard and gives remarkable flexibility, so I will continue with that.
All source code for these posts can easily be found at my Github.
